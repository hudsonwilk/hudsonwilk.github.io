About Us

Where Kids Play,
Grow & Thrive

At East Dallas Athletics Camp (EDAC), our mission is to create a safe, fun, and active environment to remind kids that the only screen they need in the summer is sunscreen.

Campers spend the week building athletic skills, confidence, and teamwork through a variety of sports—basketball, soccer, dodgeball, kickball, volleyball, spikeball, wiffleball, putter golf, and more—with a focus on fundamentals, agility, and overall athletic development.

Meet the Founders

The Guys Behind EDAC

Lifelong friends, East Dallas natives, and passionate about giving back to their community.

Hudson Wilk

Co-Founder

UT Austin — Advertising & Business Marketing. Woodrow Wilson HS Varsity Baseball Captain, IB Diploma recipient, and 4-year NHS member.

Growing up in East Dallas through Mockingbird Elementary, William B. Travis, and Woodrow Wilson HS, Hudson's upbringing shaped who he is today. Over the past few summers, working at various camps inspired the idea to start EDAC with his dear friend Logan. His main goal is to provide an unforgettable experience to the younger generation—not only a safe, enjoyable environment during the long summer months but also mentorship, helping kids develop through a fun and inclusive process.

Logan Mabry

Co-Founder

Colorado State University — Finance. Woodrow Wilson Varsity Basketball Captain, NHS member, former lifeguard at Tietze Pool.

Logan grew up through Mockingbird, J.L Long, and Woodrow, where sports camps were always a huge factor in his life. The idea of starting a camp came about last summer, and he knew he and Hudson had a great opportunity to give back. His driving point is to allow summer fun for kids and free time for busy working parents—providing life-long memories and opportunities that will make an everlasting impact on the future generations of East Dallas.
